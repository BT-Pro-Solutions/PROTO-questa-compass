Claim Listing

Who can claim a listing?

Provider partners who are authorized to manage listings on behalf of an organization may claim an unclaimed resource. You should only claim listings your organization operates or officially represents.

What happens after you claim?

Your claim is linked to your provider account and appears in My Listings with a claim-submitted status. You will not be able to edit the listing until Questa staff completes their review.

Questa staff review

Questa staff will approve or deny your claim request. If approved, you can manage the listing from your provider portal. Any edits you make after approval will also be reviewed by Questa staff before going live in Compass.

Claim requested

Your claim has been submitted. Questa staff will review your request and update the listing status in your provider portal.
